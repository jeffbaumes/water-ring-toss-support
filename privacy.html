Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

Simple Summary: Water Ring Toss does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is completely protected.

Information Collection and Use

Water Ring Toss is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or data about you.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or statistics
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No device information
  • No crash reports or diagnostics
  • No game progress or scores

Device Motion Sensors

Water Ring Toss uses your device's gyroscope and accelerometer sensors to provide tilt-based game controls. This sensor data:

  • Is processed locally on your device only
  • Is never stored or saved
  • Is never transmitted off your device
  • Is only used during active gameplay

Third-Party Services

Water Ring Toss does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions completely offline and does not connect to any external servers.

Data Storage

The app may store minimal local preferences (such as game settings) on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any information from anyone, including children, this app is safe for users of all ages. We are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
