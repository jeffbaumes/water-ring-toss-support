Water Ring Toss Support

Need help with Water Ring Toss? Check out our frequently asked questions below, or contact us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play the game?
Simply tilt your device to control the water flow and guide the floating rings onto the posts. The game uses your device's gyroscope for natural, responsive controls. Swipe left or right to change between different tank layouts.
The controls aren't responding. What should I do?
Make sure you've granted the app permission to access Motion & Orientation. Go to Settings → Water Ring Toss → and ensure Motion & Orientation is enabled. If the problem persists, try restarting the app or your device.
Can I play without tilting my device?
Currently, the game is designed to be played using tilt controls for the most authentic experience. Touch controls may be added in a future update based on user feedback.
Does the app require an internet connection?
No! Water Ring Toss works completely offline. Play anywhere, anytime without needing Wi-Fi or cellular data.
Are there any in-app purchases or ads?
No. Water Ring Toss has no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions. What you download is the complete game with all features included.
Does the app collect my data?
No. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.
Will there be more levels or features?
We're always working on improvements! Future updates may include additional tank layouts, new challenges, and quality-of-life features based on user feedback.
Can I play on iPad?
Yes! Water Ring Toss works on both iPhone and iPad devices running iOS 14.0 or later.
